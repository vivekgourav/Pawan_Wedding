Our Story

Far far away, behind the word mountains, far from the countries Vokalia and Consonantia, there live the blind texts.

  • The Beginning:

    A serendipitous encounter in the digital realm.

    Once upon a time, in the bustling world of online matchmaking, two hearts found their perfect match. In July 2022, We connected through a matrimonial site. Little did we know that this virtual connection would lead to a love story filled with adventure, laughter, and a journey towards a beautiful Indian wedding. Late-night calls became our secret enchantment, weaving a tapestry of love and longing. It was clear that destiny was at play, drawing us closer with each passing day

  • Oktoberfest Bliss:

    A momentous occasion in time.

    As summer turned to autumn, a surprise awaited. Komal, a master planner, whisked me away to Munich's Oktoberfest and our first trip to Bavaria. Amidst the lively music and delicious food, your eyes finally met, igniting a love that had only existed in your dreams. With each passing season, our love story deepened. Visits to each other's lands, shared passions, and adventures solidified our bond.

  • Love Beyond KOMPArison:

    A journey of connection and shared adventures.

    With each passing day, your relationship grew stronger, and the decision to take it to the next level became evident. We decided to embark on more shared adventures, exotic travels, and the warmth of each other's friends and families.

    And so, our love story unfolded, a tale of two souls who met online, built a connection in the real world, and journeyed through life together. Our Indian wedding was just around the corner, a celebration of love that had transcended boundaries and brought two hearts together from different corners of the world.
